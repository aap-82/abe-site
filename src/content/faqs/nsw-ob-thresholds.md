question: What's the difference between the $10,000 and $20,000 thresholds?
answer: |
  The $10,000 threshold triggers the permit requirement. The $20,000 threshold
  adds the education requirement on top — you must complete the approved course
  (or hold an equivalent qualification) when work is valued at $20,000 or more.

The **$10,000 threshold** triggers the permit requirement. The **$20,000
threshold** adds the education requirement on top — you must complete the
approved course (or hold an equivalent qualification) when work is valued at
$20,000 or more.
