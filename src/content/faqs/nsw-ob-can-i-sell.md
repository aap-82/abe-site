question: Can I sell my home after I've finished?

  Building Act 1989 that pass to the next owner.

**Yes** — but if you sell within **7 years and 6 months** of the permit being
issued, the contract of sale must include a consumer warning that an
owner-builder permit was issued.

If the warning is missing, the buyer can void the contract before settlement.
You also carry statutory warranties under the
[Home Building Act 1989](https://legislation.nsw.gov.au/view/html/inforce/current/act-1989-147)
that pass to the next owner.
