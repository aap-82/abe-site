---
question: What can't I do under an Owner Builder Permit?
answer: |
  You can't do specialist work like electrical wiring, plumbing, drainage,
  gasfitting, or air-conditioning and refrigeration unless you hold the
  relevant trade licence. Those tasks must be done by an appropriately
  licensed tradesperson — and you must use a written contract if their work
  exceeds $5,000.

You **can't** do specialist work like:

- Electrical wiring
- Plumbing, drainage, gasfitting
- Air-conditioning and refrigeration

…unless you hold the relevant trade licence. Those tasks must be done by an
appropriately licensed tradesperson — and you must use a **written contract**
if their work exceeds $5,000.
