Warwick Smith is Senior Consultant, Compliance and Operations at Corporate Development Resource
Group (CDRG), with over 27 years of senior experience across the Australian vocational education
and training sector.

Warwick's career spans both regulator-facing and provider-facing roles in VET. He has served as
Chief Executive Officer of Training Aid Australia, Chief Executive Officer of the Australian
Institute of Compliance Professionals, and Compliance Manager at TasCollege. Across those
appointments he has built and overseen quality systems for Registered Training Organisations,
designed compliance frameworks aligned with the Standards for RTOs, and guided organisations
through ASQA audits.

Through CDRG, Warwick now consults independently to RTOs, education partners, and industry
bodies on operational design, compliance review, and currency assurance.

For ABE Education, Warwick performs independent compliance and currency review of published
Owner Builder course pages. His role is distinct from course content development — that work
is performed by Dominic Ogburn (licensed NSW builder, ABE Education CEO).
