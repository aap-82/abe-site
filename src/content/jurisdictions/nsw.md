## NSW Owner Builder requirements

Anyone undertaking residential building work in NSW valued over **$10,000** must
hold an Owner Builder Permit. Permits are administered by **Building Commission NSW**
under the Home Building Act 1989, with applications submitted through Service NSW.

When the value of the work reaches **$20,000 or more**, applicants must also have
completed the approved education — five nationally recognised units of competency
delivered by a Registered Training Organisation — or hold an equivalent qualification.
