## NSW Owner Builder requirements

Anyone undertaking residential building work in NSW with a value over $10,000
must hold an Owner Builder Permit issued by NSW Fair Trading. To qualify for
the permit you must complete an approved Owner Builder course and submit a
permit application with the prescribed fee.

This page is a regulatory summary. For the course content see the
[NSW Owner Builder Course](/courses/nsw-owner-builder-course/).
